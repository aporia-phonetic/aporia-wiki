# The Meridian House

`world_id`: `the_meridian_house`

Five strangers are summoned to a sealed mid-century estate on the night it is scheduled for demolition. Inside, the walls breathe, the clocks run backward, and a single locked room at the center of the house emits a low, continuous tone that each person hears differently. Before dawn, they must agree on one collective decision — or the house decides for them.
