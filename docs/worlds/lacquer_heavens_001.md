# The Lacquer Heavens

`world_id`: `lacquer_heavens_001`

A vast celestial empire suspended above a drowned world, where divine ministries govern the flow of fate through ink, seals, and sacred ledgers. Mortal petitioners climb impossible towers to plead their cases before immortal magistrates, while a growing schism between the Ministry of Endings and the Ministry of Births threatens to unravel the architecture of time itself.
