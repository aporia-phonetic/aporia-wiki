# The Verdant Deep

`world_id`: `verdant_deep`

A vast, ancient jungle concealing the ruins of a pre-human civilization. The crew of a deep-science expedition navigates biological Resonance schools, a city that may still be alive, and the secrets each member carried in. Pulp adventure register: danger, wonder, and the weight of what is found.
