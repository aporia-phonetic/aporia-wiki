# The Cinderfield Compact

`world_id`: `cinderfield_compact_001`

A vast coal-and-iron nation where the ruling Compact of industrialists has harnessed the souls of the dead as fuel for their infernal engines. Workers, called Ashborn, toil in smog-choked foundries above mass graves. A conspiracy of grief, class war, and stolen memory ignites when a factory inspector discovers her own death certificate pre-signed and dated six weeks in the future.

## Genre & tone

- **Genre:** industrial_gothic_thriller
- oppressive and soot-stained
- desperately human amid machinery
- conspiratorial and gaslit
- mournful with buried rage
